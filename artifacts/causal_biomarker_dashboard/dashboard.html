üî¨ Methodology

Causal Discovery Methods:

  • NOTEARS: Neural-oriented causal discovery for observational data
  • PC-MCI: Temporal causal discovery with conditional independence testing
  • Mendelian Randomization: Genetic instrument-based causal inference

Integrated Scoring Components:

  • Causal Evidence (30%): Confidence in causal relationships
  • Statistical Significance (25%): P-value based scoring
  • Effect Size (15%): Magnitude of biomarker association
  • Pathway Centrality (10%): Position in causal network
  • Clinical Actionability (5%): Therapeutic potential
  • Other factors (15%): Association strength, temporal stability
