## Mission & Outcomes
**Mission.** Discover mechanistic, translatable biomarkers by actively learning causal structure across **molecule ‚Üí cell state ‚Üí tissue function ‚Üí clinical outcome**, using patient-derived, perfused, multicellular in-vitro systems.

**Success Outcomes**
- Biomarkers with **interventional evidence** on tissue function, **replication** across donors and models, and **clinical mediation** in harmonized cohorts.
- **Patient avatars** that forecast functional responses and support **personalized biomarkers** and **donor-specific treatment simulations**.
- A **closed loop** that continuously improves knowledge (and the KG) with every data cycle.

	‚Ä¢	Evidence: prior score, posterior updates, uncertainty, direction/sign, cell/tissue context, condition tags, source/license, versioning.

	‚Ä¢	Prior & constraint for modeling (regularizes causal search and module discovery).

	‚Ä¢	Representation learning: multimodal contrastive/self-supervised across imaging, function, omics.

Decision & Orchestration
	‚Ä¢	Active learning/Bayesian optimization: choose perturbations (dose/order/timing) that maximize expected causal information and reduce uncertainty.
	‚Ä¢	Model predictive control (MPC): enforce viability/safety constraints while exploring.
	‚Ä¢	Scheduler: resource-aware execution across devices/assays with replication and counterbalancing.

Biomarker Discovery Pipeline
	‚Ä¢	Use cases: diagnostic, prognostic, predictive, monitoring, PD markers.

	‚Ä¢	Evidence dossier: mechanism path (graph), interventional evidence, replication, clinical mediation, analytical validation, risk/uncertainty, next steps.

‚∏ª

Patient Avatars & Personalization

Evidence Ladder & Validation

	‚Ä¢	Analytical validation: LoD/LoQ, linearity, precision, robustness.
	‚Ä¢	Biological validation: orthogonal assays, rescue experiments, donor hold-outs.
	‚Ä¢	Transportability: explicit checks from in-vitro to clinical domain.

## Glossary

### üî¨ **Core Concepts**
- **Functional mediator** ‚Äî tissue-level readout (e.g., TEER, MEA) enforced as the bridge from molecular state to clinical outcome.
- **Digital twin (avatar)** ‚Äî donor-specific hybrid mechanistic‚ÄìML model calibrated to that donor's functional/omic trajectories.
- **Evidence ladder** ‚Äî progression from correlation ‚Üí interventional causality ‚Üí clinical mediation ‚Üí prospective validation.
- **KG** ‚Äî typed, versioned graph encoding biology, context, priors, and live evidence with uncertainty and provenance.

### üß¨ **Biomarker Types**
- **Diagnostic** ‚Äî identifies current disease state or condition
- **Prognostic** ‚Äî predicts future clinical outcomes
- **Predictive** ‚Äî forecasts response to specific treatments
- **Monitoring** ‚Äî tracks disease progression or treatment response
- **Pharmacodynamic (PD)** ‚Äî measures drug mechanism and effect

### üéØ **Validation Levels**
- **E0: Correlation** ‚Äî Statistical association only
- **E1: Temporal** ‚Äî Time-ordered precedence with module linkage  
- **E2: Interventional** ‚Äî Causal evidence from in-vitro perturbations
- **E3: Replication** ‚Äî Cross-model and cross-donor validation
- **E4: Clinical** ‚Äî Clinical association with functional mediation
- **E5: Prospective** ‚Äî Prospective validation with analytical rigor

### üîß **Technical Terms**
- **TEER** ‚Äî Trans-epithelial electrical resistance (barrier function)
- **MEA** ‚Äî Multi-electrode array (electrophysiology)
- **OMOP** ‚Äî Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership (data standard)
- **PBPK** ‚Äî Physiologically-based pharmacokinetic modeling
- **QSP** ‚Äî Quantitative systems pharmacology
- **CRISPRi/a** ‚Äî CRISPR interference/activation for perturbations

### üìä **Analytics & AI**
- **Causal Discovery** ‚Äî Automated identification of cause-effect relationships
- **Federated Learning** ‚Äî Distributed ML training without data sharing
- **Active Learning** ‚Äî Intelligent experiment design to maximize information gain
- **Uncertainty Quantification** ‚Äî Systematic assessment of prediction confidence
- **Multi-modal Fusion** ‚Äî Integration of diverse data types (omics, imaging, function)
